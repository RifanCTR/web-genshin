Raiden Shogun

Raiden Shogun is a character from Genshin Impact, a 2020 action role-playing gacha game developed by miHoYo. Added in the game's 2021 update, he was originally a boss enemy that players had to fight, later released as a playable character.

Shenhe

Shenhe is a playable Cryo character in Genshin Impact. The daughter of an unnamed exorcist couple, Shenhe was taken in and raised by Cloud Retainer as a student after a traumatic event triggered by Shenhe's father during her childhood.

Diluc

Diluc Ragnvindr is a playable Pyro character in Genshin Impact. Born into the wealthy Ragnvindr Clan, Diluc is the current owner of Dawn Winery and a highly respected noble in Mondstadt.
